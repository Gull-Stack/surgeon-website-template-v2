Meet Your Surgeon

Dr. Jane Smith, MD, FACS

Double board-certified plastic surgeon with over 20 years of experience and more than 15,000 procedures performed. Dr. Smith combines surgical precision with an artistic eye to deliver natural, beautiful results.

After graduating magna cum laude from Harvard University, Dr. Smith earned her Doctor of Medicine at Johns Hopkins School of Medicine — one of the most prestigious medical schools in the world. She completed her general surgery residency at UCLA Medical Center and her plastic surgery fellowship at NYU Langone Health, training under some of the most renowned plastic surgeons in the country.

Today, Dr. Smith is recognized as one of America's best plastic surgeons by Newsweek and has been named a Castle Connolly Top Doctor for 10 consecutive years. Her practice in Beverly Hills serves patients from across Southern California and around the world.
